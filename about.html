About Team X

We are a collective of cybersecurity enthusiasts dedicated to advancing digital security through research, education, and competition.

Our Mission

At Team X, we believe in making cybersecurity knowledge accessible to everyone. Our mission is to:

  • Document and explain complex security concepts in approachable ways
  • Participate in CTF competitions to test and expand our skills
  • Contribute to the cybersecurity community through open-source tools
  • Mentor the next generation of security professionals

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Team X began as a small group of university students passionate about cybersecurity. What started as casual participation in local CTF events has grown into an internationally recognized team competing in major competitions.

Along the way, we've discovered that our greatest impact comes not just from solving challenges, but from sharing our knowledge with the community. This realization led us to create this platform for comprehensive writeups and educational resources.
